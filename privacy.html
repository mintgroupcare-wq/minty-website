⚠10. EMERGENCY DISCLAIMER

Mint Group Private Care provides nonmedical private duty home care. Our services are designed to assist clients with companionship, personal care, daily living activities, and other nonmedical support.

Mint Group Private Care is not an emergency response provider and does not provide emergency medical services. If a client experiences chest pain, difficulty breathing, loss of consciousness, signs of a stroke, severe bleeding, a serious fall, or any other life-threatening emergency: call 911 immediately.

Do not rely on email, website forms, or other nonemergency methods to request urgent assistance. If immediate medical care is needed, contact your local emergency services or go to the nearest emergency department.

For urgent concerns regarding scheduled services, please contact Mint Group Private Care directly during business hours.
