🌿 WHO WE SERVE

Nonmedical Private Duty Care for Adults (18+) and Seniors

Who We Serve

✅ Seniors aging in place
✅ Adults with disabilities
✅ Adults recovering from illness, injury, or surgery
✅ Individuals needing companionship or personal care
✅ Family caregivers needing respite

Services We Do Not Provide

❌ Childcare or babysitting
❌ Skilled nursing or medical procedures
❌ Emergency medical services

If a client's needs change and require skilled nursing or emergency medical care, we will encourage the family to contact the appropriate healthcare provider or emergency services.

🚗 EXPANDED SERVICE

Transportation & Community Outings

Beyond appointments and errands, we help clients stay connected to the people, places, and activities they love.

EXAMPLES INCLUDE:

✅ Medical and dental appointments
✅ Grocery shopping and pharmacy visits
✅ Banking and errands
✅ Visits to parks, senior centers, or places of worship
✅ Social events, shopping trips, and family visits

🚙 Transportation Services: Transportation is available for clients who can safely transfer into a standard passenger vehicle. Clients who require transportation while remaining seated in a wheelchair may need a wheelchair accessible transportation provider.
