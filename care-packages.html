🌿 CARE OPTIONS

Choose the Care That Fits Your Needs

Every client has different needs. Mint Group Private Care offers both occasional visits and ongoing care services.

🌿

As Needed Care (PRN)

Ideal for clients and families who need occasional assistance without committing to a regular schedule.

EXAMPLES INCLUDE:

  • Post surgery recovery
  • Family caregiver respite
  • Transportation to appointments
  • Short term recovery support
  • One time overnight care
  • Assistance during vacations or emergencies

Visits are scheduled based on availability. No long term commitment is required.

🌿

Ongoing Care Services

Designed for clients who would benefit from consistent, recurring care.

EXAMPLES INCLUDE:

  • Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday
  • Weekday morning care
  • Weekend assistance
  • Overnight care
  • Customized weekly schedules

BENEFITS INCLUDE:

  • Personalized Care Plan
  • Written Service Agreement
  • Consistent caregiver
  • Priority scheduling
  • Regular care updates
  • Ongoing communication with family

Ending Services: Either the client or Mint Group Private Care may discontinue services with reasonable notice. Immediate termination may occur when necessary for client or caregiver safety, nonpayment, unsafe working conditions, or other circumstances that make continuing services inappropriate.
